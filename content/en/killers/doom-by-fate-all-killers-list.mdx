The Nightmare is the king of psychological warfare. His signature catchphrase, "Go to sleep," isn't just for show—it signals a powerful debuff that slows survivors and blurs their vision.

### The Scorcher
The Scorcher is perhaps the most technical character on the **doom by fate all killers list**. He utilizes a "Heat" mechanic that requires constant management. If his heat level gets too high, he deals massive area-of-effect damage, but if it drops too low, his movement speed significantly decreases.

*   **Heat Management:** Players must balance using abilities to generate heat without overheating and stalling out.
*   **Burn Damage:** Survivors hit by The Scorcher take damage over time, making him excellent at "hit and run" tactics.
