## Countering the New Killer: Survivor Survival Guide

Facing 1x1x1x1 can be overwhelming due to the visual clutter and teleportation mechanics. However, there are several ways to mitigate the killer's effectiveness.

*   **Node Cleansing:** Survivors can "Reboot" Glitch Nodes by standing near them for 4 seconds. This removes the debuff zone but alerts the killer to your location.
*   **Breaking Line of Sight:** Since 1x1x1x1's Glitch Jump requires a brief wind-up, breaking line of sight can cause the killer to overshoot their teleport.
*   **Audio Tracking:** While the static is non-directional, the sound of the "Void Protocol" charging has a distinct high-pitched whine. Listen for this to know when a teleport is imminent.
