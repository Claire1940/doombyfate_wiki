## Strategies for Playing as 1x1x1x1

To dominate as **doom by fate 1x1x1x1**, you must lean into his role as a spatial hunter. He is not designed for "camping" a single spot but rather for patrolling the map with high efficiency.

1.  **Utilize the Glitch:** Use your natural visual distortion to confuse survivors during loops. By flickering your movement, you can trick survivors into vaulting directly into your path.
2.  **Master the Rift:** Your 'Q' skill (Dimensional Rift) should be used to cut off paths rather than just closing distance. Predict where a survivor is heading and rift to that exit point.
3.  **Pressure the 3D Realm:** Since 1x1x1x1 harbors hatred for the 3D realm, his skills often involve breaking the environment. Use your abilities to destroy pallets or interactable objects faster than other killers.
4.  **Aura Reading:** Save your 'R' skill for when you lose track of a survivor near a completed objective. The "Badlands Call" is essential for finding those hiding in the shadows.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid over-relying on your teleport skills in open areas. The cooldown can leave you vulnerable to being "looped" by experienced survivors who know how to exploit the downtime between your abilities.
