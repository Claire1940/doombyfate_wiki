## The Old Man: Character Overview & Lore

The entity known as The Old Man, or SCP-106, is a legendary figure within the SCP (Secure, Contain, Protect) universe. In the lore of *Doom By Fate*, he was once a captive of the Foundation until the mysterious Bloxwatchers offered him a deal. In exchange for his freedom from containment, he agreed to participate in the Game of Torment, serving as a primary executioner for their twisted entertainment.

Visually, The Old Man is a nightmare of advanced decomposition. He is covered in a black, tar-like substance that serves as the source of his corrosive powers. Interestingly, he is the first killer in the game to feature fully modeled fingers, adding a layer of uncanny realism to his "Sinister Grasp." His protruding ribcage and permanent, haunting smile signal to all survivors that escape is statistically unlikely once he sets his sights on a target.

## Abilities and Combat Mechanics

To play **doom by fate the old man** effectively, you must master his kit of five primary abilities. Unlike "Brawler" style killers who rely on brute force, The Old Man utilizes damage-over-time (DoT) and crowd control.

| Ability Name | Description | Primary Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Claw** | A standard melee strike using his tar-covered hands. | Deals immediate damage and applies a minor corrosion debuff. |
| **Pocket Dimension** | Drags a survivor into a separate, decaying realm. | Stacks damage each time a survivor is re-captured (+1 per instance). |
| **Sinister Grasp** | A precision grab that prevents survivor movement. | Roots the target in place for a short duration. |
| **Portal Latch** | Creates a gateway through solid surfaces or floors. | Allows for high-speed traversal across the map. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** While Corroded Frenzy deals significant AoE damage, it has a long cooldown. Using it too early in a chase can leave you vulnerable to stuns from Soldiers or Brawlers.

### The Power of the Pocket Dimension
The defining trait of **doom by fate the old man** is his ability to isolate players. When a survivor is pulled into the Pocket Dimension, they enter a "zone" where they must find a specific exit door to return to the main map. 

The most dangerous aspect of this ability is the **stacking damage mechanic**. Every time a specific survivor is pulled into the dimension during a single match, the base damage they take while inside increases by +1. This means that targeting the same individual repeatedly will eventually make it impossible for them to find an exit before their health bar hits zero.

## Strategic Playstyle: How to Dominate as The Old Man

Playing as The Old Man requires a shift in mindset from traditional "slasher" gameplay. You are a predator that thrives on stealth and psychological pressure. Follow these expert strategies to maximize your kill count in 2026:

### 1. Prioritize High-Value Targets
Do not waste time chasing healthy "Soldier" class survivors who are looking for a fight. Instead, use your Corrosive Frenzy or Portal Latch to surprise "Engineers" or anyone currently working on generators. A survivor focused on a skill check is the easiest target for a Pocket Dimension grab.

### 2. The "Tunneling" Meta
While often frowned upon in casual play, "tunneling" (repeatedly targeting one player) is mathematically the most effective way to use The Old Man. Because the Pocket Dimension damage stacks, you can effectively "delete" a player from the match by the third or fourth capture, as the tick damage becomes too high for them to survive the exit search.

### 3. Mastering the Portal Latch
The Portal Latch is not just for movement; it is for mind games. In maps like **Crossroads**, survivors often feel safe behind walls or on second-story balconies. By placing a portal on the ceiling or floor below them, you can bypass their defenses and emerge directly behind them.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Always keep one portal near a cluster of generators. This allows you to patrol the map and return instantly if you hear a generator being repaired.

## Survivor Guide: How to Counter The Old Man

Facing **doom by fate the old man** is a test of nerves. If you find yourself in a match against him, your primary goal is to minimize the time you spend alone.

### Team Coordination
The Old Man excels at picking off isolated targets. By staying in groups of two or three, you increase the chance that a teammate can stun the killer if he attempts a Sinister Grasp or Pocket Dimension pull. Soldiers and Brawlers should always lead the way, as they possess the defensive stats to survive an initial encounter.

### Escaping the Dimension
If you are pulled into the Pocket Dimension:
1. **Don't Panic:** Look for the visual cues of the "true" exit door.
2. **Use Stuns Immediately:** If you have a stun ability (like the Soldier’s bash), save it for the moment you emerge from the dimension. The Old Man will often be "camping" the exit point to immediately recapturing you.
3. **Healing Priority:** After escaping, your first priority must be finding a Medkit or a teammate to heal you. Low health makes you a magnet for his Corroded Frenzy.

### Map Awareness
On maps with limited verticality, The Old Man is much easier to track. However, in complex maps, listen for the sound of "bubbling tar." This audio cue indicates that he is nearby or using a portal. If the sound grows louder, move away from the nearest wall or floor grate immediately.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Doom By Fate The Old Man based on SCP-106?**

A: Yes, the character is a direct homage to SCP-106 from the SCP Foundation universe, sharing his signature black tar appearance, corrosive abilities, and pocket dimension.

**Q: How do you escape the Pocket Dimension in Doom By Fate?**

A: To escape, you must navigate through a series of rooms or corridors within the dimension and interact with the correct door. Choosing the wrong door may reset your progress or deal additional damage.

**Q: Can The Old Man walk through walls?**

A: While he cannot walk through walls passively, his **Portal Latch** ability allows him to place portals on surfaces, effectively letting him teleport through walls, floors, and ceilings to ambush survivors.

**Q: What is the best survivor class to counter The Old Man?**

A: The **Soldier** and **Brawler** classes are highly effective. Their ability to stun the killer can interrupt his grab animations and provide enough time for teammates to escape his AoE attacks.
