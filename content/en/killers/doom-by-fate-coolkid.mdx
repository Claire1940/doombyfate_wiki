## Advanced Combo Sequences and Strategy

The hallmark of a skilled **doom by fate coolkid** player is the ability to execute the "One-Shot Combo." While this sequence is technically a series of moves, the speed at which they occur makes it nearly impossible for most survivors (specifically Bacons) to react.

### The Standard Execution Combo

For most survivors, the following sequence provides a guaranteed kill if the first hit connects:

1.  **Binding Slash (E):** Start with this to apply a heavy slow.
2.  **Lunatic Strike (R):** Follow up immediately while the survivor is slowed; the unblockable nature ensures the 45 damage hits.
3.  **Dual Piercer (Q):** As they ragdoll or try to run, throw the projectiles to finish their remaining HP.
4.  **M1 (Basic Slash):** If they are somehow still standing, a quick basic attack will finish the job.

| Target Type | Strategy Adjustment |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Bacon** | Punish flashlight usage immediately with **Binding Slash**. They are fragile and usually die to the base combo. |
| **Soldier** | Be careful; they have higher health and can block. Use **Lunatic Strike** to bypass their block entirely. |
| **Jukers** | Use **Dual Piercer** to force them out of loops. The projectiles go through obstacles, making "safe" spots dangerous. |

When facing a Soldier, a common tactic for the **doom by fate coolkid** is to bait out a block. Soldiers will often try to block the *Dual Piercer* projectiles. If you use *Lunatic Strike* right as their block animation ends, the lingering slowdown from their defensive stance makes them a sitting duck for your heaviest hit.

## Counterplay: How to Survive the Hunt

If you find yourself on the other side of the blade, surviving a **doom by fate coolkid** requires intense coordination. Because the killer is designed to punish solo players, sticking together is your best defense.

1.  **Flashlight Management:** As a Bacon, save your flashlight specifically to cancel his *Speed Hack*. Interrupting his momentum is the only way to prevent a quick gap-close.
2.  **Double Blocking:** As a Soldier, if you block the first sword of *Dual Piercer*, do not let go of the block button. You must block the second sword immediately to avoid the slowness that sets up his *Lunatic Strike*.
3.  **Environmental Awareness:** Since *Dual Piercer* goes through walls, never assume you are safe behind a thin partition. Stay mobile and try to zig-zag to make his projectiles miss.

## Lore and Origins

The **doom by fate coolkid** is unique among the roster as he is the only character based on an actual Roblox historical figure—specifically the alternative account of the hacker 007n7. According to the in-game lore, after 007n7 disappeared, his fanatics split into three factions: Team CK2, Team CK3, and Team CK4. 

The killer we play in-game represents these fanatics. This is why there are three distinct models:
*   **CK-2 "Maskey":** Wears a smiling red mask.
*   **CK-3 "Smiley":** Features a raised mask with a wider grin.
*   **CK-4 "Shiesty":** Wears a rugged veil.

The chase theme, "Tercet Turmoil," reflects this trio by featuring three different voices in the lyrics. It is widely theorized by the community that the C000lkid we control has eventually hunted down and eliminated the other two fanatics to claim the title of "Leader," as evidenced by the Milestone 3 skin which features the masks of the other two hanging from his waist.
