## Survivor Strategies: Surviving the Heat

When the **doom by fate third massacre** music begins to swell, survivors must transition from objective-focused play to pure survival. The "Heat" mentioned in the OST refers to the mounting pressure as the round timer winds down or the killer gains momentum.

### Essential Survival Tips
1.  **Stick Together (With Caution):** While grouping up allows you to finish generators faster and provide mutual protection, it makes you a massive target for Brute-type killers who deal heavy area-of-effect damage.
2.  **Utilize Terrain:** Loops and obstacles are your best friends. Use high-vault areas to break the line of sight, especially against Rushdown killers who rely on straight-line speed.
3.  **Listen for Audio Cues:** Beyond the **doom by fate third massacre** track, listen for footsteps, breathing, and the sound of abilities powering up. Most stuns can be dodged if you react to the audio wind-up.
4.  **Objective Priority:** Lowering the round time by completing "gens" is the only way to escape. Do not get caught in a "looping" trap where you distract the killer but no progress is being made on the map.
