## FAQ

**Q: Do skins in Doom by Fate provide any gameplay advantages?**

A: No, skins in *Doom by Fate* are purely cosmetic and do not provide any gameplay advantages or affect character stats. The only rare exceptions might be minor hitbox adjustments on some character models, but these are generally not designed to give a competitive edge.

**Q: How do I get more Tix to buy the Doom by Fate best skins to buy?**

A: You can acquire more Tix by playing and winning games in *Doom by Fate*. Additionally, developers occasionally release promotional codes that can be redeemed for Tix or other in-game rewards.

**Q: What are Milestone skins and how do I earn them?**

A: Milestone skins are special cosmetics unlocked by leveling up a specific Killer or Survivor. There are four tiers: Level 5, 15, 25, and 50. By consistently playing your chosen character, you will naturally progress towards unlocking these exclusive skins.

**Q: Are Seasonal skins available all the time?**

A: No, Seasonal skins are limited-time cosmetics released to celebrate specific events or holidays, such as Christmas or Halloween. They are only obtainable during the event window and may or may not be reissued in future events, making them quite rare.
