## Understanding the Skin System in Doom By Fate

Before diving into the specific Project Moon crossovers, it is important to understand how cosmetics function within the game. Skins are primarily cosmetic items that alter a character's appearance without directly impacting gameplay stats or power levels. However, higher-tier skins often include "fluff" features that enhance the feel of the game, such as custom voice lines, unique weapon models, and exclusive chase music.

The game categorizes skins into several distinct tiers, each with its own acquisition method:

| Skin Category | Acquisition Method | Rarity/Availability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Skins** | Purchased in the Shop using **Tix** | Always available; rotates occasionally |
| **Milestone Skins** | Leveling up specific Survivors or Killers | Permanent unlocks at specific levels |

## How to Earn Tix for Project Moon Skins

Since the **doom by fate project moon skins** like Second Kindred require a significant investment of 3,500 Tix, players need efficient ways to farm this currency. Tix is earned through active gameplay, though the rate can vary based on performance.

1. **Complete Daily Missions:** These are the most consistent source of Tix. Most missions involve surviving for a specific amount of time or achieving a set number of takedowns as a Killer.
2. **Performance Bonuses:** Finishing a match as the "MVP" or the "Last Man Standing" provides a multiplier to your Tix rewards.
3. **Event Participation:** Seasonal events often have separate currency tracks that can be converted into Tix or offer high-value skins as direct rewards.
4. **Milestone Leveling:** As you level up your characters, you occasionally receive Tix bundles alongside your milestone skins.
