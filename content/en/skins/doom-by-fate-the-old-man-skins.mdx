### How to Play The Old Man: Strategies for Success

To effectively wield The Old Man's powers, focus on surprise and attrition:

*   **Embrace Stealth:** Unlike some killers, The Old Man benefits greatly from stealth. Avoid overusing abilities like Corroded Frenzy in open areas, as it can alert survivors. Catching people off guard is key to initiating your Pocket Dimension grabs, which are your primary means of eliminating targets.
*   **Leverage Pocket Dimension Stacking:** This is The Old Man's signature move. Kidnapping the same survivor multiple times into your Pocket Dimension causes the damage effect to stack, increasing by +1 each time. This cumulative damage can quickly reach a point where survivors cannot escape in time, making repeat targets highly vulnerable.
*   **Prioritize Vulnerable Targets:** Look for survivors with lower health or those preoccupied with objectives like generators. These are ideal targets for your Pocket Dimension, as they are less likely to react quickly or escape once pulled in.
*   **Mind Your Exits:** After leaving the Pocket Dimension, you are vulnerable. Survivors, especially Soldiers and Brawlers, will often anticipate your return and wait to stun you. Try to exit in less conspicuous locations or be prepared to immediately engage or disengage. Maps with limited cover, like Crossroads, can make this particularly challenging.

> **💡 Tip:** Practice using your Pocket Dimension on different survivor types. Understanding their escape mechanics will help you time your grabs and maximize the stacking damage. Don't be afraid to retreat if an initial grab fails; reposition and strike again.
