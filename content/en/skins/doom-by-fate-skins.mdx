**Q: Are seasonal Doom By Fate skins gone forever if I miss them?**

A: Seasonal skins are limited-time cosmetics, but they *may* be reissued during future events or holidays. However, this is not guaranteed, so it's always best to acquire them when they are first available if you truly want them.

**Q: What are Milestone Skins and how do I unlock them?**

A: Milestone skins are special **Doom By Fate skins** unlocked by reaching specific level tiers with a particular Survivor or Killer. For example, you can unlock Milestone I at Level 5, Milestone II at Level 15, Milestone III at Level 25, and Milestone IV at Level 50 for that character.
