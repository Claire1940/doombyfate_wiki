Positioning is often overlooked by newer players who focus solely on their hotbar. Most bosses have a "dead zone" behind them where their primary attacks cannot reach. However, the **dbf roblox wiki** notes that many bosses in the 2026 update have been given "tail swipes" or "back-blasts" to counter this specific strategy. 

1.  **Maintain Mid-Range:** This allows you to react to both melee swipes and long-range projectiles.
2.  **Use the Vertical Space:** Many of the "Dumb" bosses have hitboxes that don't extend very high. Using double jumps or flight items can bypass many ground-based hazards.
3.  **Coordinate Aggro:** In multiplayer sessions, ensure that the player with the highest health (the "Tank") stays in front of the boss while others attack from the flanks.
