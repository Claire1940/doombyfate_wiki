## Safety Recommendations for Players

While the developers have since removed the most hazardous effects of the 1x1x1x1 entity, players should still exercise caution. The **death by fate wiki** suggests the following steps to ensure a safe gaming environment:

*   **Adjust Brightness:** Lower your monitor or device brightness to 50% when playing horror titles.
