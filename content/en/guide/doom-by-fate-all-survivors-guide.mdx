## Survivor Classes and Roles

In Doom by Fate, survivors are categorized into specific roles that define their contribution to the team. Understanding these roles is essential for building a balanced lobby.

| Class | Primary Focus | Key Characteristics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Survivalist** | Objectives & Stealth | High utility, ability to track killers, and self-preservation tools. |
| **Sentinel** | Protection & Disruption | High HP, stuns, and knockback abilities to peel for teammates. |
| **Support** | Healing & Buffing | Lower HP but essential for keeping the team alive through medical kits. |

## Doom by Fate All Survivors Guide: Character Breakdown

Each survivor has a distinct personality and toolkit. Below is the detailed analysis of the current roster as of April 2026.

### Bacon (The Survivalist)
Bacon is the default character every player starts with. Despite being "free," his kit is surprisingly versatile for players who enjoy utility. He is a **Survivalist** with **100 HP**.

*   **Ability 1: Flashlight:** Blinds and slows the killer when used at close range. 
*   **Passive: Battery:** The flashlight consumes 10% battery per second. You can recharge 30% of the battery for every generator you successfully complete.
*   **Ability 2: Pizza:** A self-heal that restores **20 HP**. Note that you must stand perfectly still while eating, or the move cancels and goes on cooldown without healing you.
*   **Ability 3: Tracker:** Pings the killer’s location every 1.5 seconds, showing you exactly how far away they are.

### Brawler (The Sentinel)
The Brawler is a frontline disruptor designed to get in the killer's face. He has **110 HP** and costs **500 Tickets**.

*   **E Move: Pocket Sand:** A two-part ability. First, you scoop up sand (which prevents stamina regeneration). Second, you throw it to stagger, immobilize, and knock back the killer.
*   **R Move: Liver Blow:** A powerful melee strike that deals roughly **50 HP** damage to the killer, making it one of the strongest offensive moves in the survivor's arsenal.

### Jane Doe (The Support)
Jane Doe is currently the only pure healer in the game. With a fragile **90 HP**, she requires careful positioning but is vital for team longevity. She costs **450 Tickets**.

*   **Q Move: Syringe:** Stabs the killer to deal minor damage and apply a significant slow effect.
*   **E Move: Medkit:** A targeted heal that restores **35 HP** to a teammate. Be careful: if the target uses an ability while being healed, the process cancels.
*   **R Move: Patch Up:** A self-healing syringe that restores **35 HP** to Jane herself.

### Jester (The Trickster Sentinel)
Jester is a hybrid between a sentinel and a trapper. He has **100 HP** and excels at creating "safe zones" for his team. He costs **450 Tickets**.

*   **Q Move: Hat Trap:** Places a hat on the ground. Any killer walking near it has their sprint disabled. These traps last 15 seconds unless destroyed.
*   **E Move: Banana Trap:** Drops banana peels that cause the killer to slip and trip. Pressing the button twice can act as an immediate close-up stun.
*   **R Move: Pie:** A projectile that follows your camera's direction. If it hits the killer, they are slowed, partially blinded, and unable to attack for a short duration.

### Soldier (The Meta Sentinel)
Arguably the strongest character in the current version of the game, the Soldier boasts a massive **150 HP** and a kit designed to punish aggressive killers. He costs **450 Tickets**.

*   **Passive: Iron Will:** The Soldier never limps, regardless of how low his health is. This hides his current HP status from the killer.
*   **Q Move: Carry:** Allows the Soldier to pick up and carry a teammate to safety.
*   **E Move: Block:** A 2-3 second defensive stance. If the killer hits you during the block, they are knocked back and you gain a speed boost. A third successful block results in a powerful counter-punch.
*   **R Move: Shotgun:** Fires a pellet from his spear that stuns, staggers, and knocks the killer back. It has a generous hitbox but a significant cooldown.

## Best Strategies for Survival

Navigating the maps in [Doom by Fate on Roblox](https://www.roblox.com/games/14235347583/Doom-By-Fate) requires more than just knowing your buttons. Follow these expert tips to increase your win rate:

1.  **Chain Your Stuns:** If you are playing with a Jester and a Soldier, coordinate your traps. A killer slipping on a banana peel is the perfect target for a Soldier's shotgun blast.
2.  **The "Pizza" Bait:** As Bacon, you can bait the killer into thinking you are vulnerable while eating pizza. If they lunge, cancel the animation and use your flashlight to blind them mid-swing.
3.  **Prioritize Jane Doe:** If you see a Jane Doe on your team, the Sentinel must stay near her. She is the killer's primary target because her healing can negate all the killer's progress.
4.  **Master the Block:** The Soldier's block is predictive. Since many killer attacks have a "wind-up" animation, learn the timing to trigger your counter-punch consistently.

> **Warning:** Be careful when using the Brawler's Pocket Sand. Picking up the sand temporarily halts your stamina regeneration, which can leave you stranded if the killer is already in mid-chase.

## How to Unlock New Survivors

Tickets are the primary currency in Doom by Fate. You can earn them through several methods:
*   **Completing Rounds:** Winning a match as a survivor grants a significant ticket bonus.
*   **Performance Milestones:** Staying in a chase for a long duration or completing multiple generators rewards extra currency.
*   **Daily Codes:** The developers frequently release codes that can be redeemed in the main menu for 200-500 tickets.

While it may be tempting to buy a Killer first, most veteran players suggest unlocking a high-tier survivor like **Soldier** or **Jane Doe** first to ensure you can farm tickets more efficiently in high-level lobbies.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best character in the Doom by Fate all survivors guide?**

A: Currently, the **Soldier** is considered the best for solo players due to his 150 HP and powerful block mechanic. For team play, **Jane Doe** is essential as the only source of external healing.

**Q: Can Bacon's flashlight work through walls?**

A: There is a known mechanic where the flashlight's "blind" radius can clip through thin walls or corners, allowing you to slow the killer without being in their direct line of sight.

**Q: How do I recharge the flashlight battery?**

A: Bacon recharges 30% of his flashlight battery for every generator completed by the team. It does not recharge passively over time.

**Q: What does the "Sentinel" class actually do?**

A: Sentinels are designed to "peel" for the team. This means their abilities are focused on stopping the killer from chasing a specific target, usually through stuns, knockbacks, or physical blocking.
