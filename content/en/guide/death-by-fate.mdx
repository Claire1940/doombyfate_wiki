If you are playing high-intensity games like *Doomed by Fate*, follow these steps to ensure a safe experience:

1.  **Check for Warnings:** Look for a splash screen at the start of the game that mentions flashing lights or "Photosensitivity Warnings."
2.  **Adjust In-Game Settings:** Many games now include an "Epilepsy Filter" or "Reduced Motion" mode in the settings menu.
3.  **Monitor Your Environment:** Play in a well-lit room and maintain a safe distance from the screen to reduce the impact of flickering lights.
4.  **Listen to Your Body:** If you feel dizzy, nauseous, or experience muscle twitches, close the game immediately.
