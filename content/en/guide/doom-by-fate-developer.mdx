### Key Inconsistencies in the Story

2.  **Hospital Access:** Mel, a 13-year-old, claimed to have snuck into a restricted surgical ward and watched the procedure through a window at midnight. Modern hospitals use strict key-card access and do not allow unaccompanied minors into active surgery wings.
3.  **The Neighbor:** The story involved a 19-year-old neighbor finding Kit and texting a 13-year-old at midnight instead of calling emergency services immediately.
4.  **The Timeline:** The transition from the seizure to the hospital and subsequent death occurred in a window of time that didn't align with standard emergency response and surgical prep.
