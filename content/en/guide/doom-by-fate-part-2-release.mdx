## New Killers: The Old Man and The Priest

The **doom by fate part 2 release** wouldn't be complete without the introduction of new threats. The most notable addition is **The Old Man**, a killer that players have identified as being heavily inspired by SCP-106. Unlike previous killers who rely on raw speed, The Old Man utilizes the "Undetectable" status to catch survivors off-guard.

### The Old Man (SCP-106)
This killer specializes in area denial and stealth. With the ability to phase through the environment and apply "Slowness," he is a nightmare in tight maps like the Mansion or the Mystery Hotel.

*   **Core Mechanic:** Escaped from containment, utilizing entropy and decay.
*   **Passive:** Movement speed is slightly lower, but footsteps are muffled.
*   **Tactical Advantage:** Can bypass traditional survivor loops by applying the new Slowness status effect.

### The Priest
Introduced shortly before the Part 2 climax, The Priest has received significant balance tweaks in the latest patch. His kit revolves around "Shriek" and "Crucifixion," which now has a significantly increased hitbox to make it more viable in open-field chases.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** When playing against The Priest, remember that his "Snap" ability can now be blocked and countered. Timing your block as a Soldier is key to negating his slowness debuff.

## New Status Effects and Combat Mechanics

One of the deepest changes in the **doom by fate part 2 release** is the formal introduction of status effects. These mechanics add a layer of "Damage over Time" (DoT) that forces survivors to manage their health more carefully.

| Status Effect | Description | Duration/Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Burning** | Deals fire damage over time. | 1 dmg/s (Variable duration) |
| **Bleeding** | Applied by blades; prevents health regen. | 1 dmg/s (Up to 10s) |
| **Undetectable** | Killer loses their Terror Radius. | Stealth-based |
| **Slowness** | Reduces survivor walkspeed significantly. | Applied by Snap/Old Man |

These effects have been integrated into existing killer kits. For example, Jeff the Killer’s "Sweet Dreams" and "Nighty Nights" abilities now apply Bleeding for 5 and 10 seconds, respectively. This ensures that even if a survivor escapes a chase, they remain vulnerable for a significant period.

## Map Reworks and Removals

As the Alpha stage concludes with the **doom by fate part 2 release**, the developers have cleaned up the map pool to ensure better performance and gameplay flow.

*   **Mystery Hotel:** Added as a primary map for Part 2, featuring multiple floors and tight corridors perfect for The Old Man.
*   **Rampaged Ship:** Updated with a new objective system to prevent "stalling" by survivors.
*   **Removals:** The "Jolly Winter" and "Mystery Hotel" maps were temporarily removed in undocumented follow-up updates to address performance glitches, though they are expected to return in a "Doomed" variant later in 2026.

## Major Killer Rebalances

To maintain a competitive environment, several veteran killers received massive overhauls in the 2026 Part 2 update.

### C000lkidd
*   **Stamina:** Reduced from 110 to 100 to prevent infinite chasing.
*   **Binding Slash:** Now does 10 base damage plus 5 burn damage.
*   **Lunatic Strike:** Reworked to deal 35 damage when used alone, but only 20 when comboed with Binding Slash.

### Jeff The Killer
*   **Rage Cooldown:** Significantly increased from 15s to 68s. This prevents Jeff from being in a near-permanent state of unblockable attacks.
*   **Bloodshed:** Now applies a 3-second Bleeding effect but has added endlag, making it riskier to miss.

### John Doe
*   **Corroded Wave:** Cooldown decreased to 28s, but the spikes move slower, giving survivors more time to react.
*   **Command Grab:** Damage reduced to 24.5 to align with other "grab" type abilities.

> **Warning:** Farming in private servers has been officially disabled as of this update. To earn Tix and EXP for the new skins, you must play in public matchmaking.
