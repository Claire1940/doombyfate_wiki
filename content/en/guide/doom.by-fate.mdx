## Safety Updates and Epilepsy Warnings

### Recommended Settings for New Players

1.  **Epilepsy Filter:** Toggle this to "ON" to reduce the frequency of high-speed strobe effects during "The One" encounters.
2.  **Brightness Scaling:** Set this to a comfortable level (recommended 0.8) to prevent eye strain during dark-to-light transitions.
3.  **Motion Blur:** Disabling this can help reduce motion sickness, which is common in the game's fast-paced chase sequences.

> **💡 Tip:** If you feel dizzy or disoriented during gameplay, look away from the screen immediately. The game's "Astronomical" mode is designed to be disorienting, so taking regular breaks is highly recommended.
