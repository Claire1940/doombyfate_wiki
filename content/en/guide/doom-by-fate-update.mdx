## Character Spotlight: Martyr and Chara

The highlight of the **doom by fate update** is undoubtedly the introduction of two legendary-tier characters. Both Martyr and Chara bring unique playstyles that disrupt the existing "tank-heavy" meta seen in early 2026.

### Martyr: The Selfless Avenger

## The New Titles System Explained

2. **Event Completion:** Participate in the 1x1x1x1 event to earn limited-edition titles like "Glitch Hunter."

## Advanced Combat Tactics for the 1x1x1x1 Meta

The **doom by fate update** has significantly altered how players approach mid-range combat. With the introduction of the 1x1x1x1 boss mechanics, environmental awareness has become just as important as mechanical skill.

### Mastering the Glitch Mechanic
During matches on the new event map, "Glitch Zones" will randomly appear. Standing in these zones provides a temporary 15% boost to ability cooldown speeds but drains 1% of your health per second. 

Follow these steps to maximize the benefit of Glitch Zones:
*   Identify the zone spawn by the purple static on the ground.
*   Lure your opponent toward the edge of the zone.
*   Use a knockback ability (like Chara's "Slash") to pin them inside the zone while you stay on the perimeter.
*   Only enter the zone when your Ultimate is at 90% charge to quickly top it off for a finishing blow.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the Martyr character in the new doom by fate update?**

A: Martyr can be unlocked by completing the "Path of the Penitent" questline or by purchasing the character bundle in the in-game shop using Fate Points.

**Q: Are the new Titles permanent once unlocked?**

A: Yes, once you have met the requirements and claimed a title, it remains in your inventory permanently, even after the 1x1x1x1 event ends.

**Q: Why is Chara considered a "glass cannon"?**

A: Chara has the highest base damage in the **doom by fate update**, but their health pool is roughly 30% lower than other fighters, making them very susceptible to burst damage if you miss a parry.

**Q: Can I use the 1x1x1x1 title on any character?**

A: Yes, Titles are account-wide. Once unlocked, you can equip them regardless of which character you are currently playing as.
