## Understanding Survivor Roles: Survivalists and Sentinels

In *Doom By Fate*, survivors aren't just generic characters; they fall into distinct classes that dictate their primary function within a match. Currently, the two main roles identified are **Survivalists** and **Sentinels**, each offering unique advantages to the team. Mastering these roles and understanding how they complement each other is key to a truly survivor-sided team dynamic.

### Survivalists: The Objective Specialists

Survivalists are the backbone of objective completion. Their kit is designed to help them efficiently complete tasks, heal themselves, and gather crucial information about the killer's location. A prime example of a Survivalist is **Bacon**, who excels at generator repair, has self-healing capabilities, and can pinpoint the killer's whereabouts. These characters are essential for pushing the game forward and ensuring that escape objectives are met.

### Sentinels: The Frontline Disruptors

Sentinels, on the other hand, are the team's frontline. They specialize in interrupting the killer, providing crucial support, and protecting their teammates. Their abilities often include stuns, displacement tools, and other mechanics that can peel the killer off an injured ally or buy precious time during a chase. A well-played Sentinel can significantly extend the life of their teammates, making them invaluable in high-pressure situations.

1.  **Prioritize Objectives:** Focus on completing generators and other escape requirements.
2.  **Communicate Effectively:** Call out killer locations, downed teammates, and objective progress.
3.  **Utilize Roles:** Survivalists focus on gens, while Sentinels peel and disrupt.

## Advanced Strategies for Survivor Success

To truly dominate as a survivor in *Doom By Fate* and leverage the game's potential to be survivor-sided, consider implementing these advanced strategies:

### Generator Rush and Map Control
Focusing on generators early and efficiently is paramount. Divide and conquer: have multiple survivors working on different generators simultaneously. Survivalists like Bacon are ideal for this. Simultaneously, keep an eye on the killer's movements. If the killer is committed to one side of the map, survivors on the opposite side can make significant progress.

### Looping and Kiting
If you find yourself in a chase, learn the map layouts to effectively "loop" the killer around obstacles, windows, and pallets. This buys crucial time for your teammates to complete objectives or stage a rescue. Sentinels can assist by stunning the killer or creating distance with their displacement tools. Never give up on a chase too early; every second you waste the killer's time is a victory for your team.

### Strategic Healing and Rescues
Knowing when and how to heal or rescue a downed teammate is vital. Sometimes, it's better to let a teammate be hooked if it means completing the last generator. However, a well-timed rescue, especially with a Sentinel's peel abilities, can turn a losing situation around. Always assess the risk-reward before attempting a dangerous rescue.
