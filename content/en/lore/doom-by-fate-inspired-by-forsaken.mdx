## Character Lore and Abilities: A Nod to Roblox's Past

One of the most compelling aspects of *Doom By Fate* is its rich character roster, which cleverly intertwines elements of old Roblox lore and internet creepypastas. The game features a variety of Survivors and Killers, each with distinct abilities and backstories that significantly enhance the gameplay experience.

For Survivors, characters like **Jane Doe** stand out. As the designated Medic, Jane's kit is centered around healing, with abilities like "Patch Up" for self-healing and "Set Health" to remotely heal allies. What's more, she's not defenseless; her "Syringe" ability allows her to slow down Killers with a melee attack, making her a formidable Combat Medic. **Brawler** offers a more direct approach, capable of stunning Killers with a "Liver Blow" or blinding them with sand.
